Crossing Red-Helios is a storytelling and worldbuilding project set in a universe designed using the game Space Engineers. The primary story of CRH is released in a graphic novel comprised of in-game screenshots, with supplementary trailer videos and cinematic re-enactments of scenes from the graphic novel itself.

CRH is also a collaborative community project. Players of Space Engineers are free to join the project and contribute to its lore and content.

The Story of Andrei Grisson

A former smuggler who worked to bring supplies and technology from the northern systems down to the frontier. His career came to an abrupt end during the infamous NDR military operation known as "Severed Artery" in which the NDR destroyed the hidden network of deep space jump gates used to covertly travel to the northern regions of Dracona.

A Fight For Freedom

200 years after the signing of the Myca accord, the Frontier is threatened once more by outside powers seeking to control the region. The innumerable factions of these border systems remain splintered, yet rumors have begun circulating that new organisation calling itself the "Red-Helios Alliance" is working to unite the Frontier and repel the northern invasions.

New Draconic Republic

Successor to the Six Suns Federation [SSF], considered to have been the greatest of the major powers for much of the history of the Draconis Cluster. The NDR is often cited as the envy of Draconans, with a stable society and safe systems. Yet the republic possesses a highly nationalistic view and a closed immigration policy, leaving the rest of the cluster to fend for itself against the chaos of stellar history.
